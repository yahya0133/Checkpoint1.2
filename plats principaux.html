Pizza

Ingredients: Dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, bell peppers.

Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, meat (chicken or lamb), spices, saffron.

Sushi

Ingredients: Sushi rice, fresh fish (like salmon), nori (seaweed), vegetables.

Couscous

Ingredients: Semolina, vegetables, meat (lamb or chicken), spices.

Tacos

Ingredients: Taco shells, ground meat, cheese, vegetables.

Fried Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, flour, spices, oil for frying.

Pasta (Spaghetti)

Ingredients: Spaghetti, tomato sauce, ground meat, Parmesan cheese.

Momo (Dumplings)

Ingredients: Flour, ground meat, vegetables, spices.
